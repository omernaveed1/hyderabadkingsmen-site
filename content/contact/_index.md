This is an independent Pakistan cricket publication. We welcome:

- **Story tips** about the PSL, Pakistan cricket, or Sindh / Hyderabad cricket scene
- **Corrections** to anything we have written
- **Partnerships** with cricket publications, podcasts, content creators, or community accounts
- **Advertising** inquiries (we accept select brand partnerships)
- **Acquisition inquiries** from the official Hyderabad Kingsmen franchise, the PCB, PSL, or other authorized rights holders interested in taking ownership of the domain, the content, and the publishing infrastructure

For all of the above, email us at **contact@hyderabadkingsmen.com**.

We aim to respond within 48 hours during the PSL season and within 5 days in the off-season.

## For corrections

Please include the URL of the post and the specific sentence or fact in question. We publish corrections at the bottom of the relevant post with a dated note.

## For rights holders

If you are with the **Hyderabad Kingsmen franchise**, the **FKS consortium ownership**, the **PCB**, the **PSL**, or another authorized cricket organization, and you have an interest in:

- Acquiring this domain and publication
- Establishing an official partnership
- Discussing licensing or co-branding
- Reporting a trademark concern

…please email us and we will respond promptly. We have built this site as a community asset and we are open to transferring it to the right official owner under reasonable terms.

## About us

This is an independent cricket publication, not affiliated with the Hyderabad Kingsmen franchise or any other cricket governing body. For more, see the [About page](/about/) and the [Editorial Disclaimer](/disclaimer/).
