This Privacy Policy explains how Hyderabad Kingsmen ("we", "our", "the site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit hyderabadkingsmen.com.

## What we collect

We use **Google Analytics 4** to understand how visitors use the site. This collects anonymous aggregate data: pages visited, time on page, country, device type, referring source. We do not collect names, emails, or any personal information through analytics.

We use **Google AdSense** to display ads (once approved). AdSense and its third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

We do not maintain a mailing list. We do not collect comments or user-generated content.

## Cookies

This site uses cookies set by Google Analytics and Google AdSense. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without losing access to any site content. We do not use cookies for advertising on our own behalf.

## Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Third-party links

Posts on this site frequently link to external sources (ESPNcricinfo, the PCB, news sites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.
