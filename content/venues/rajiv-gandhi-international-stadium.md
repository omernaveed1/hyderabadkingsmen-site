The Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium in Uppal, on the eastern edge of Hyderabad, is the home of Sunrisers Hyderabad and one of the most batting-friendly grounds in the IPL. If you have only watched IPL games here, you would assume it has always been a flat highway. The reality is more interesting.

## The pitch

Uppal's surface used to behave like a typical sub-continental pitch — some early seam, plenty of turn after the eighth over, and a slowness that brought spinners into the game. Test cricket here favoured spinners (ask Ravichandran Ashwin, who has 24 wickets in 4 Tests at the ground). For the first ten IPL seasons it was a fair contest between bat and ball.

That changed around 2022. The curator switched to a different soil composition and the pitches started playing harder and truer, with less turn and more carry. By IPL 2024 it was the most batting-friendly venue in India outside Chinnaswamy. SRH's 287 against MI in 2024 set a venue record that nobody, not even SRH themselves, has come close to matching since — though MI's chase of 277 here in 2024 came within 11 runs.

## Dimensions and how they play

The square boundaries are 65 metres on the ad-side and 68 metres on the pavilion side. The straight boundary is 73 metres. These are not particularly short for Indian grounds, which makes the high scores even more impressive. Klaasen and Head do not need short boundaries to clear the rope; they just need a true surface and a still evening.

The big tactical wrinkle is the dew. Uppal evenings in March, April and May get heavily dewy by the 35th over of an innings, which kills spinners and makes the ball skid onto the bat. The team batting second under lights is favoured by roughly 8 per cent in first-innings-equivalent terms. Captains who win the toss here in the IPL almost always bowl first.

## Notable matches

**SRH 287/3 vs MI, March 2024.** The Travis Head & Heinrich Klaasen show. Head 102 off 41, [Klaasen 80 not out off 34](/players/heinrich-klaasen/). SRH won by 31 runs in a game where Mumbai chased like their lives depended on it.

**MI 277/8 vs SRH, March 2024 (return fixture).** The reply game. Tilak Varma 64, Ishan Kishan 69, but Bhuvneshwar Kumar's two wickets in the 18th over swung it back to SRH.

**India vs Australia, ODI World Cup 2023.** The non-IPL highlight of the venue in recent years. India won by 6 wickets in a game where Virat Kohli made 85 and Ravindra Jadeja took 3-28.

## If you are going to a game

Gate F is the closest to the metro. The Uppal Metro stop is a 12-minute walk from the stadium concourse and is by far the easiest way in — parking is limited and the traffic on Warangal Highway is brutal on match days. Arrive at least 90 minutes before first ball for the security check, longer if SRH are playing CSK or RCB. The food inside is improving; outside Gate D is a row of biryani stalls that are better than anything in the stadium.
