## Sources

Our match reports, statistics, and player information are derived from publicly available sources, including:

- ESPNcricinfo
- The official PCB website
- The official PSL website
- Reputable Pakistani cricket publications (Cricwick, Geo Super, Samaa, Dawn sports)
- Official team and player social media accounts

We cite specific sources at the bottom of each post. Where we make analytical claims, the analysis is our own original work.

## Accuracy

We make every effort to ensure factual accuracy. Cricket scorecards, match results, dates, and team selections are checked against official sources at the time of publication.

That said, mistakes happen. If you find an error, please [contact us](/contact/) and we will publish a correction. For corrections, please include the post URL and the specific sentence or fact in question.

We do not publish unverified rumours as fact. Where we discuss transfer speculation or unconfirmed reports, we attribute the report to its source and clearly indicate it is unconfirmed.

## Opinions

Posts on this site frequently contain editorial opinion and analysis. Where we offer an opinion (about a player's form, a captain's decision, a team's strategy), it is the writer's view and not a statement of fact.

We welcome respectful disagreement.

## Not professional advice

Nothing on this site constitutes professional, legal, financial, or betting advice. We do not endorse cricket betting and we strongly advise against gambling on cricket matches.

## Affiliate, advertising, and AI-assisted content

This site displays advertising via Google AdSense (once approved). We do not control the specific ads shown. We are not paid to write favourably about any player, team, brand, or product. If we ever enter a paid partnership (sponsored post), we will clearly label such content as "Sponsored" or "Partner content" at the top of the post.

Some posts on this site are drafted with the assistance of editorial AI tooling under human editorial direction. Every post is reviewed by a human before publication. We do not consider AI-assisted drafting incompatible with original analysis; the analytical angle, the sourcing, and the editorial responsibility remain ours.
